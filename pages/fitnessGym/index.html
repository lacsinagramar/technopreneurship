Fitness Gym Membership and Trainer Scheduling with Billing and Payment System

Landing Page

This is the landing page of the FiGS.

Dashboard

This is where the quick reports come to play, like the distribution of customer types, who's the latest person purchased a membership, the amount of people every month, and all other variables.

Members

This displays the regular members who are active, and utilizing the gym.

Freezed Accounts

This, on the other hand, displays freezed accounts.
Freezed account means that the client applied a "pause" for their account so that the remaining time in their membership will not be wasted. Admins can freeze/unfreeze with prior notice from the clients.

Trainers

Displays current trainers in the gym and their specialization, schedule, age, branch assigned, and their contact number.

Personal Training

Displays the current personal training (PT) sessions along with their trainer-in-charge, slot count, and specialty of the session.

Promo

Displays current offers that the gym present occassionally, and their benefits and bonuses.

Discount

Displays the discounted factors based on the customer's status that affects the pricing of the rates.

Branches

Displays branches available as stated in the system.

Event

Displays booked, and on-going events that take place inside the gym.

Membership Requests

This displays a list of members that applied in the system. The admin will be the one in charge for the member's approval.

Payment

Displays the member's paid amount, due amount, and the due date of the payment.

System Description

For the system name, figs are sacred fruits that represent peace and abundance, while its seeds represent faith, fertility, understanding, and knowledge. This fruit also refers to Adam and Eve from the Garden of Eden.


For naming convenience, FiGS is much easier for the programmers to refer their work than saying the entire system name.


FiGS is a fitness gym membership, trainer assignment, with billing and payment system. A software program that caters gym users (admins, trainers, staff, and their clients) to ease their daily transactions.


The system can grant membership, as well as freezing, and transferring of the customer's account to another successor. This system can recommend a set to you based on your input. You can also inquire a trainer based on your workout preferences. And lastly, this can record every transaction made, and generate report based on the data received.
