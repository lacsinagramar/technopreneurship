Product Description

Nowadays, organic products are underrated and most people uses and consumes products that have chemicals and other artificial ingredients in it. Little do they know that by consuming non-organic products can affect their health. Organic and All-natural products is what we all need because not only it has 0% of chemicals and other artificial ingredients that may harm us, but it can help our local producers by consuming more natural grown products. To help promote Organic and All-natural products, we created an Organic Shop Online Ordering and Consignment System.

Organic Shop Online Ordering and Consignment System are made to help local producers of organic products and also to promote organic products to the public. This system will be able to update the Supplier about the sales and inventory of their product. Also, applying to consign is made easier because it's now automated and it's just done by clicking and typing necessary informations. We ensure that all informations are secured. For the customers, this system will be easy to use since it is user and mobile friendly. The customer can just choose whatever he/she desires, then after inputting necessary details, the order will now be processed and evaluated.
