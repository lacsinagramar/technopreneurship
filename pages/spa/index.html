Product Description

Most of us have had a stressful day and sometimes, we just want to take a day off, relax, pamper ourselves. Going to a spa is a perfect place to spend your day off with! But making reservations can be full of inconvenience sometimes and with that, the stress level just increases. And so, we made the Online Booking and Spa Management System so customers can make reservations without hassle.

Online Booking and Spa Management System aims to provide a hassle-free processing of transactions. With the features of this system, customers can go to the spa and not worry about the long process of making a reservation. Front desk officers can also do their jobs easier since they can utilize the use of this sytem because there are features provided for them. It will make them more productive.
