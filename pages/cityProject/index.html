Product Description

City Project Application and Beneficiary Releasing Management System is to systematize the processes when applying for a project and releasing benefits that is offered by the office of the Representative. It is a web-based system where residents can apply for different projects, and request projects that is currently what the residents need.

City Project Application and Beneficiary Releasing Management System can be helpful when it implements to the government municipality. It will lessen or prevented double application and overload of data, handling of different projects and applications are easier, also online passing of requirements can be done here so going to their office is not a problem anymore. Also scheduling is more organized than before.

Features

  • Computerized application form and upload of requirements for easier transaction.

  • Easier to navigate project details and requirements.

  • To reduce the amount of time and effort for inquiring details.

  • Easier communication through the use of messaging.

  • Organized scheduling of passing requirements and releasing of benefits.
