Product Description

Because of the ever growing idea of new technologies being made for the ease of people here on Earth, a lot of people loved the idea of being in for the industry. And for that matter, different companies look for things that would make an improvement and impact unto their lives while managing their own. For that same reason, a system may also be made. A system that would help a company change their processes into a more fast-paced one and easily managed is a great help for people who don't have too much time on their hands.

This system has that goal to help tugboat companies see that a system for them is a big help on managing their own. It is fast-paced, can be accessed anywhere, it is new and on the trend, it can easily monitor, record and schedule a job order, it also has that function to manage its consignee contracts online or just a few clicks away. With all of the benefits it may give to a tugboat company, it is really a good option to try having one like this. Scheduling a Job Order, Managing Bills and Payments, Monitoring and Managing of Contracts would be faster and easier. More time unto the consignee and manager of the company may be accumulated. Thus, be a good change.

"It is not that bad to have too much time on your hands and be happy."

Features

  • Online management and monitoring of contracts made with consignees.

  • Scheduling with the use of a calendar for Dispatch and Hauling Services offered by the tugboat company.

  • Messaging that would be helpful for the consignee, affiliates and the tugboat company itself.

  • Monitoring of locations where the tugboats will be and estimation as to how much time it would take a tugboat to arrive at the destination ports.

  • Notifications on reminders about payments, billing, new schedule, new contracts etc are present.
