Product Description

The Jeepney is one of the most popular, accessible and cheapest medium of transportation in the Philippines, and even considered a national identity. However, some operators are facing a problem in running their business cause they can't perform their processes properly all the time. So we decided to develop a system that will help the jeepney operator to solve their problems.

The Jeepney and Driver Dispatching and Inspection with Collection System aims to provide platform, which will help the jeepney operators and other staffs/employees to perform processes in the business. This will cover schedulling of trips, monitoring of units and also collection processes. It also includes driver and unit acquisition, tracking of driver's offenses and auto supplies management.
