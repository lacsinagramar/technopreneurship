Product Description

Many dreams have been broken, many talents gets wasted, and all of this happen because of the fact that not all the people had given the chance to study at schools they want, due to financial problem. And this triggers many people to make a scholarship program to solve the problem that limits people to rise and shine, and by supporting their program and make it much easy, we make a Scholarship Management System.

Scholarship Management System aims to provide platforms, which will help the coordinator in all the processes involved in the scholarship. This will support all the paper and requirements that will be submitted online by the applicant or scholar. And we ensure that all the records/information are kept securely. And we also aim to provide an up to date monitoring and updating of all the scholar or applicant information so that the coordinator can easily track all the scholars status and complaint. Through this, the scholarship program can help many aspiring scholars.
